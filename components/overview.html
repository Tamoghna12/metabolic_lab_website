About the Lab

Metabolic Engineering for Sustainable Solutions

Led by Dr. Ahsan Islam, our lab integrates metabolic engineering, synthetic biology, and computational systems biology to develop sustainable bioprocesses for chemical production, environmental remediation, and industrial biotechnology.

01

Metabolic Systems Analysis

Using genome-scale constraint-based metabolic models to understand and optimize microbial metabolism for sustainable production of platform chemicals and biofuels.

02

Gas Fermentation

Engineering acetogenic and other C1-gas fermenting organisms for efficient conversion of synthesis gas (CO, CO₂, H₂) into valuable chemicals and fuels.

03

Environmental Biotechnology

Developing bioremediation solutions using engineered microorganisms for chloride-containing compound degradation and environmental cleanup.
