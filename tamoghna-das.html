Tamoghna Das

PhD Researcher

Tamoghna Das is a passionate PhD researcher at Loughborough University, supported by the prestigious UKRI Scholarship. His research focuses on developing digital twins of bioprocesses to optimize media and feeding strategies for industrial cell culture processes. With a strong foundation in biochemical engineering and data science, Tamoghna combines high-throughput screening techniques with advanced multivariate data analysis to create predictive computational models that bridge the gap between theoretical understanding and practical industrial applications.

Research Vision

Tamoghna's research vision is to revolutionize industrial bioprocessing through the integration of digital twin technology and advanced data analytics. By creating predictive models that can accurately simulate bioprocess behavior, his work aims to reduce development timelines, improve product quality, and enable more sustainable and cost-effective biomanufacturing processes. The ultimate goal is to bridge the gap between laboratory-scale research and industrial-scale implementation, making advanced bioprocess optimization accessible to a wider range of industries and applications.
