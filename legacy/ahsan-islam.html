Dr. Ahsan Islam

Principal Investigator
Senior Lecturer in Biochemical Engineering

Dr. Ahsan Islam is a leading researcher in metabolic engineering and synthetic biology with extensive international experience spanning MIT, University of Toronto, and Imperial College London. With over 15 years of research experience and 30+ publications in high-impact journals, he specializes in constraint-based metabolic modeling, genome-scale analysis, and microbial production of platform chemicals. His work bridges fundamental scientific discovery with practical industrial applications.

Research Philosophy

Dr. Islam's research philosophy centers on the integration of computational and experimental approaches to solve complex biological challenges. He believes in bridging the gap between fundamental scientific understanding and practical industrial applications, with a strong emphasis on sustainability and innovation. His work aims to develop next-generation biological systems that can address global challenges in energy, health, and environment.
