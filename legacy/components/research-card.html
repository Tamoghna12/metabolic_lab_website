Research Areas

Our work spans three core disciplines at the intersection of biology and artificial intelligence

Artificial Intelligence

Developing machine learning algorithms for biological data analysis, protein structure prediction, and pathway optimization. Our AI models accelerate discovery and predict biological behavior with unprecedented accuracy.

  • Deep learning for genomics
  • Natural language processing for literature mining
  • Predictive modeling of biological systems

Metabolic Engineering

Engineering microbial systems to produce valuable compounds sustainably. We design novel metabolic pathways and optimize cellular factories for pharmaceutical, chemical, and fuel production.

  • Genome-scale metabolic modeling
  • Synthetic pathway design
  • Industrial bioprocess optimization

Bioinformatics

Developing computational tools for analyzing massive biological datasets. Our work includes multi-omics integration, network analysis, and visualization platforms for complex biological systems.

  • Multi-omics data integration
  • Network biology and systems analysis
  • High-throughput data processing pipelines
